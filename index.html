A versatile, award-winning photographer/visual storyteller with more than 17 years experience in photography, photo editing, writing, video, lighting, transmitting technologies, managing archives and curating photos. I have covered major global humanitarian news and documentary stories and high-level events for Reuters, AFP, The New York Times, Sipa Press, The Guardian and various other newspapers and NGOs in the United States and South Asia. I am a self-starting, multi-tasking team player, skilled in in variety of different visual approaches and formats, as well as long-form storytelling, collaborating with writers, designers, producers and multimedia team to deliver journalistically sound and visually compelling stories. I have coordinated a fast-paced, 24/7 news photo department, shot breaking news pictures, quickly and efficiently edited images with accurate captions, metadata and accompanying text by using photo management tools under constant tight deadlines. Extensive knowledge of photography, operating industry-standard cameras and lighting for shooting pictures of high-level officials, promotional, candid event shoots, photo essays and day-in-the-life pictures, using highly specialized digital and analogue equipment and obtaining location permits. I have photo-edited special projects, photo stories and several photography books. My work has been published in a wide array of national and international publications, as well as featured in exhibitions and screenings worldwide. I have also been a judge in international photo exhibitions. I have taught photography and co-founded the Counter Foto Photography Department in Bangladesh, mentoring students on their documentary photography projects. My objective is to work for a world-class platform that values high-quality visual journalism.
